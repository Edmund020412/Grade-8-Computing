A matcha latte is a creamy beverage made by whisking powdered matcha green tea with hot water and then adding steamed milk. It offers a smooth, earthy flavor with a naturally sweet and slightly vegetal finish, and can be enjoyed hot or cold. Unlike other teas, the whole leaf is consumed as a powder, resulting in a drink that is more concentrated with caffeine than traditional green tea.

3 Reasons Why Matcha Is Good For You

  1. It is rich in antioxidants that protect against cell damage
  2. It can boost brain function by improving alertness and focus
  3. It may support heart health by potentially lowering the risk of heart disease

How To Make Matcha Latte

  • Sift 2g/1 tsp of high quality matcha powder to deep wide matcha bowl.
  • Add 80ml of hot water, max 80°
  • With the bamboo whisk and a relaxed wrist vigorously froth matcha making “M” motions for about 20 secs. and until a smooth creamy emerald green foam layer is formed.
  • Prepare an iced or hot milk and pour the smooth creamy emerald green foamy matcha on the milk
  • Sit back and enjoy your cup of Zen.
